# Minimal BOM

This is a minimal component list for reproducing the triggerable high-voltage
module. It is not a complete procurement list and does not define the final
electrical safety design.

- Arduino-compatible MCU
- DC-DC high-voltage boost converter
- Triggered switching/driver module
- DC power supply for the low-voltage side
- Breadboard or custom PCB
- Resistors and jumper wires
- Insulated high-voltage wiring and connectors
- Enclosure and strain relief
- Emergency shutoff or accessible power disconnect

Verify component ratings and isolation distances before operation.
